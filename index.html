About Me

Hiya! I have basically lived in Brooklyn, New York all my life. I love to doodle, listen to chill music, write down any crazy ideas that come to mind, read sci-fi / fantasy novels (still waiting for Doors of Stone... but Patrick Rothfuss is actually my role model), play board games, wonder about the existence of morality outside a theistic framework and whether or not it could actually be just an emergent property of the natural universe, go on long walks through forests, look up new recipes to cook, and generally just relax with friends.

I have been a big fan of Pokémon since I was a child, and the Pokémon community has been immensely helpful in exposing me to new things. My interest in character design, graphic design, web development, and programming all started from my early enthusiasm in creating fan projects.

I plan on majoring in computer science, but I am also very interested in anthropology, comparative religion, environmental science, maritime studies, and a lot of other things too. I'm not going to pretend that I have my life figured out, but I am very happy with my life the way it is and hope that somehow this will rub off on the people around me.

My current projects include:

  • infographic websites directed toward young audiences
  • improving my graphic design repertoire
  • mastering pixel art
  • learning how to use Android Studio
  • developing a game that will teach playeres about sustainability and environmental conservation while still being super fun
